□ League Of Legend

Summary

Evaluate your skills in League of Legends, a 5v5 MOBA where individual affiliations and team members are formed.
Choose your array, complete your item crafting, battle it out in battle, and secure your Nexus from your enemies.
Find your own champion and achieve victory with over 150 players with infinite playstyles.

Specification

International competition

League of Legends World Championship

It is the largest LoL e-sports tournament hosted directly by Riot Games and a global tournament that marks the end of the year.
The official abbreviation is Worlds, or World Championship, and top club teams from nine regions compete for the title of world champion.
It is held in a different region every year after the regular season ends, and in 2023 it was held in South Korea.
As League of Legends has the largest e-sports market, it holds the best record in e-sports history in terms of number of simultaneously connected viewers and viewing hours, and is the world's largest e-sports competition, breaking new records every year.

□ Valorant

Summary

Show off your precise skills, awesome loot, breathtaking gameplay and thrilling experiences.
In VALORANT, attackers and defenders, each comprised of five players, compete in a best-of-13 shootout over 25 rounds.
Because transparent and fair gameplay is important above all else, Riot Games has invested heavily in building an unprecedented backend system for VALORANT.
The system boasts dedicated 128-tick servers, custom netcode, server-privileged game architecture, unique anti-cheat features, and more.

Specification

International competition

VALORANT Champions

It is the largest Valorant esports tournament hosted directly by Riot Games and a world tournament that marks the end of the year.
There are 16 participating teams in the 2021 Champions, 2022 Champions, and 2023 Champions (2021, 2022, and 2023), and the most participating teams based on the 2023 Champions are KRÜ Esports, Fnatic, DRX, and Team Liquid, which participated three times.
It was a candidate for the best e-sports competition award at The Game Awards 2022, but unfortunately did not win the award due to the enormous impact of the 2022 Worlds.

□ Fifa Online 4

Summary

FIFA Online 4 (EA Sports™ FIFA Online 4 or FIFA Online 4, hereinafter referred to as FIFA Online 4 or FO4 for short), which began service on May 17, 2018, was developed by EA Korea Studio (formerly Spearhead) under EA and developed by Nexon.
It is the sequel to FIFA Online 3. It is based on FIFA 18.
It was first revealed at the EA x Nexon New Project Media Showcase held on November 2, 2017.

Specification

International competition

Fifa Online4 eK League

The eK League is an FC online competition jointly hosted by the Korea Professional Football League and the Korea e-Sports Association and hosted by Afreeca TV.
It is a domestic e-sports competition certified by the Federation of International Football Associations (FIFA), and the league winner will receive a prize of 10 million won and the right to participate in the EA Champions Cup or FIFAe Champions Cup offline preliminaries.
Previously, some countries had run e-sports competitions with EA Sports' FIFA series or Konami's PES series, and some domestic professional teams had also recruited professional gamers. Orders were given to establish new official competitions for each league.

□ Bubble Fighter

Summary

It is a casual TPS game for all users produced by Nexon in 2008.
Uses the PathEngine pathfinding library. Familiar characters such as Dao and Disney from Crazy Arcade and Kart Rider appear, and it is a system where you can bubble enemies by shooting a water gun and earn points by approaching bubbled enemies and popping them.
If an ally gets hit by a bubble, you can save them by approaching them and helping them before they get blown up. It is a game with similar characteristics to Crazy Arcade, where you approach and explode enemies trapped in water.

Specification

International competition

Bubble fighter ChampionsCup

The Bubble Fighter Champions Cup is an open competition for the general public.
Eight teams will be selected through a non-face-to-face preliminary round, and the winner will be selected through an offline competition.
The winner will receive a limited Champions Cup medal and be included in the Hall of Fame of past Champions Cup winners.

The 13th competition was held until 2019, but is currently temporarily suspended.

□ Overwatch

Summary

It is a team-based multiplayer hyper FPS game produced by Blizzard Entertainment in the United States.
It was released in a playable form as a closed beta on October 27, 2015, and was released worldwide on PC, PS4, and Xbox One on May 24, 2016, and was released at approximately 1:00 a.m. on October 4, 2022.
The service was terminated for patching and maintenance to launch the service, and the service was later transferred to Overwatch 2 on October 5.

Specification

International competition

Overwatch League

Overwatch franchise league hosted/hosted by Blizzard Entertainment.
It was first revealed at the BlizzCon 2016 opening event.
The first season began with the 2017 preseason, and was finally abolished with the 2023 season.
It is characterized by combining globalism with professional sports elements such as regional connections and jersey-themed skins in existing e-sports competitions.
